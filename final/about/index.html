About the Technology Club

The Technology Club is a student organization focused on exploring modern technology through hands-on learning and collaborative projects. Our goal is to give students opportunities to build practical skills in areas such as computer hardware, programming, and digital problem-solving. Unlike a traditional classroom setting, the club emphasizes learning by doing. Members work together on real projects, share ideas, and develop solutions as a team. Whether you are completely new to technology or already have experience, the club is designed to be accessible and supportive for all skill levels.

What We Do


Hands-On Hardware

Members learn how computers work by exploring hardware components and building systems. This includes understanding parts like CPUs, memory, storage, and GPUs.

Skill Development

The club helps students build skills in problem-solving, teamwork, and basic programming concepts through guided activities and group projects.

Coding & Projects

Members work on small programming projects such as simple games, websites, and interactive tools using beginner-friendly coding concepts.
