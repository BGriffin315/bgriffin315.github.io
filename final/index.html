Welcome to the Technology Club!

Our goal is to explore technology through hands-on projects, teamwork, and creativity. Whether you are interested in computers, coding, or building real-world projects, this club gives you a place to learn and grow. We focus on beginner-friendly activities and collaborative projects so everyone can participate, no matter their experience level.
