What is a Mineral Oil PC

Our first project will be to build a Mineral Oil PC.
A Mineral Oil PC is a computer that is built inside of a mineral oil suspension, because of the viscous nature of the oil it doesn't conduct electricity. This allows us to build a functioning computer that appears to be underwater. (example pictured to the right)
